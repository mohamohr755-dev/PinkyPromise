Shipping Information

Everything you need to know about getting your glow delivered.

Delivery Times

We process all orders within 24 hours. Delivery times vary based on your location:

  • Cairo & Giza: 1 to 2 Business Days
  • Alexandria: 2 to 3 Business Days
  • Other Governorates: 3 to 5 Business Days

Shipping Fees

We offer FREE SHIPPING on all orders over 2,000 EGP! For orders below 2,000 EGP, standard rates apply:

  • Cairo & Giza: 60 EGP
  • Alexandria: 75 EGP
  • Other Governorates: 90 EGP

Order Tracking

Once your order is confirmed, you will receive a tracking number via WhatsApp/Email. You can also view your order status directly from your Account Dashboard.
